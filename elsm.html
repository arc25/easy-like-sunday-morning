Live every Sunday 10am Central Wake up! It's the Easy Like Sunday Morning show! Live on Youtube at "The Easy Like Sunday Morning Show!", every Sunday, 10am central time. It's the perfect show to wake up with the whole family. But don't just take our word for it! Here's what some of our most loyal viewers have to say about it...

But what is it about? The show is a funny variety show featuring conversations, skits, and cute animals! Subversive in a SpongeBob or Pee Wee Herman sort of way and silly, The Easy Like Sunday Morning Show is family friendly goofiness that we hope adults and (slightly older to teen) kids like. Some of our influences are: '90s Adult Swim, Regis and Kathy Lee, Robot Chicken, Mr. Rodgers, SNL, Monty Python, Crazy Spoon Head, and you know the rest.

Meet the Cast!

Dan

Co-host Dan is the proud nerd, interested in comic books, dungeons and dragons, 80s cartoons and more. His "Big Fat Comedy Nerd" show is a loving tribute to the art of standup.

Margie

Co-host Margie may seem more grounded and normal than her counterpart, but she is just as big a nerd as him. She also does some world-class impressions.

Ruby and Charlie

These two dogs are stars in the making! Charlie (left) is calm and sleepy, and Ruby (right) is wild and energetic. While the lovable canines are perfect opposites, they're also great friends. .

Lady Chick-Chick and friends

Professional wrestler Lady Chick-chick, Spiderman-man, Skeletor, and other kooky characters are also bound to make an appearance. They are always showing up in skits, adding a little flavor to the situation.

Cats!

You also might see some cute cats appearing in videos. Keep a look out because there are several different ones to see! They are not easy stars to work with, but they're just so darn cute.
